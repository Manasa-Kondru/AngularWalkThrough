FEW BUILT-IN PIPES

currency || date || json || lowercase || uppercase || percent || slice etc...
  1. Used to transform data before dispalying it in the browser
  2. Pipes takes data as input and transform or format that data to some form
  3. Syntax : Expression | pipename [:parameters]
  • {{name | uppercase}}
  • {{name | titlecase}}
  • {{name | lowercase}}
  • {{salary | currency}}
  • {{salary | currency :'INR'}}
  • {{salary | currency:'INR' :'code'}}
  • {{salary | currency:'INR' :'symbol'}}
  • {{person}}
  • {{person | json}}
  • {{dob}}
  • {{dob | date}}
  • {{dob | date :'dd/MM/yy'}}
  • {{dob | date :'dd/MM/yyyy'}}
  • {{num |percent}}
  • {{company | slice :2}}
  • {{company | slice :2 :7}}
  • {{wish | customPipes}}
  • {{person | customPipes :wish}}
